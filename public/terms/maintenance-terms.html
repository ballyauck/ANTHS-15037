CyberElectrix Ltd 24/7 Emergency Maintenance Service General Terms and Conditions

Important Notice: The 24/7 emergency maintenance service referenced on our website is available exclusively as part of our comprehensive maintenance contract agreements.

24/7 Emergency Maintenance Service

The 24/7 emergency maintenance service is a premium service offering available to clients who have entered into a maintenance contract with CyberElectrix Ltd. This service provides:

  • - Round-the-clock emergency response.
  • - Priority scheduling for urgent electrical issues.
  • - Dedicated emergency contact line.
  • - Rapid response team deployment.
  • - Reduced call-out fees for contract holders.

Service Requirements

To access our 24/7 emergency maintenance service, clients need to:

  • - Enter into a formal maintenance contract with CyberElectrix Ltd
  • - Maintain current contract terms
  • - Have completed an initial electrical system assessment
  • - Agree to periodic maintenance schedules as outlined in the contract

Service Availability

24/7 availability is always guaranteed once a contract is in effect, with the exception of:

  • - Non-adherence to contract obligations by client
  • - Force Majeure incidents

Emergency Response Times

General response times, subject to specific agreements, are:

  • - Critical emergencies: Within 2 hours
  • - Urgent issues: Within 6 hours
  • - Standard maintenance: Within 24 hours
