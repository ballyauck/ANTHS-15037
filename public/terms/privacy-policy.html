CyberElectrix Ltd Privacy Policy

Important Notice: We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with the Privacy Act 2020 (New Zealand).

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website.

1. What We Collect

When you use our contact form, we may collect:

  • - Your name.
  • - Your email address.
  • - Any personal information you include in your message.

2. How We Use Your Information

We collect this information for the purpose of:

  • - Responding to your inquiry.
  • - Confirming that your email address is valid via automated reply.
  • - Communicating with you about our services.

We do not use your information for marketing purposes unless you explicitly request this.

3. How We Store Your Information

Your message is delivered to our email system. We store emails securely and only for as long as reasonably necessary to respond or provide ongoing services. No personal information is stored on the website itself.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or disclose your personal information to third parties unless required by law or with your explicit consent.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • - Access the personal information we hold about you.
  • - Request corrections to that information.
  • - Request deletion, subject to legal and operational limitations.

6. Cookies & Tracking

We may use basic cookies or analytics to understand website usage. No personal information is collected unless voluntarily submitted via the contact form.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version will always be posted on our website.
